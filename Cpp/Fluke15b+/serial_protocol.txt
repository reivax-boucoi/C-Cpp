WP6 - GND
WP7 - RxD
WP8 - TxD
Communications happens at 2400,n,8,1, by sending a single byte ascii letter. These are the ones
'i' - Turn HV mark on
'q' - Turn HV mark off
'n' - Hold toggle
'd' - Get the current LCD contents as 8 bytes.

Send 'd'
Returns 8 bytes:
I1 : K M % Diode Hz F mV A
I2 : Auto AC DC V [Ohms] uF nF 
D4 : c g b d a f e dp 
D3 : c g b d a f e dp
D2 : b g c d a f e dp
D1 : b g c d a f e dp
I3 : Conti (Beeep) Hold Minus
I3 : Minus Hold Conti
I4 : Manual uA mA
